Find Optimal cut-off
The clf_rf_tuned_gsa gives us the best AUC (not a surprise, we have optimized this score). To improve the recall I am going to try to look for an optimum threshold. To calculate the optimal cut-off (true positive rate - false positive rate == 0) I use the function metrics.Find_Optimal_Cutoff. This gives me an optimal cut-off at 0.4286 If I recalculate the
